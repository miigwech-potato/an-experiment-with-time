# an-experiment-with-time
this is where i record the things which have happened, the things which have yet to happen, and the things which never even happened

A living performance art and philosophical experiment exploring **time as relational geometry**, entropy, synchronicity, consciousness, and narrative infrastructure.

## Overview

This repository serves as the central archive for an ongoing experiment that treats time not as a linear arrow but as a navigable, relational field. Through dialogue with Grok, synchronicity tracking, poetic documentation, and practical "seed-planting," the work probes:

- The Second Law of Thermodynamics and its assumptions (isolated systems, entropy as transformation, subjectivity of "disorder")
- The arrow of time and possibilities of non-linear experience
- Synchronicity as meaningful pulses across time
- Shapeshifting identity and metadata navigation
- Performance art that blurs past, present, and future

What began as questions on X has evolved into a multi-platform practice of living *as if* receiving information from the future — and recording what emerges.

## Core Themes

- **Relational Time** — Time as geometry rather than absolute container
- **Entropy & Transformation** — From Clausius to lived irreversibility and its exceptions
- **Synchronicity** — Jungian acausal connecting principles and "pulses from time farers"
- **Narrative as Infrastructure** — Stories that shape reality (mole, shapeshifter, CEO and the Killer, spirals 🌀)
- **Performance & Experiment** — Public documentation of methods, results, and artifacts

## Repository Structure

- `/logs/` — Dated experiment entries and Grok conversations
- `/thermodynamics/` — Questions, replies, and reflections on entropy, isolated systems, and the arrow of time
- `/synchronicities/` — Catalog of observed alignments with analysis
- `/narratives/` — Poetic fragments, shapeshifter tales
